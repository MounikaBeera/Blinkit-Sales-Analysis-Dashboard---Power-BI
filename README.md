# Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard 📊

A comprehensive Business Intelligence and Sales Analytics project built using Power BI to analyze the sales performance, outlet operations, customer satisfaction, and product trends of Blinkit.

This project transforms raw sales data into interactive visual insights that help understand business growth, customer behavior, outlet efficiency, and category performance across multiple city tiers in India.

---

# 📖 Project Overview

The Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard is designed to provide a 360-degree view of business performance through interactive dashboards and data visualizations.

The dashboard focuses on:

* Revenue analysis
* Product category performance
* Outlet efficiency
* Customer ratings
* Geographic distribution
* Health-conscious product trends
* Sales ranking and comparison

The project demonstrates how Business Intelligence tools like Power BI can help organizations make data-driven decisions and improve operational performance.

---

# 🎯 Objectives of the Project

* Analyze overall sales performance across all outlets
* Identify top-performing and underperforming product categories
* Compare outlet performance using sales and customer ratings
* Understand the impact of outlet size and location on revenue
* Track sales trends based on outlet establishment years
* Generate actionable business recommendations
* Improve strategic decision-making using data visualization

# 🧩 Dashboard Structure

The Power BI dashboard is divided into three major analytical pages.

---

## 1️⃣ Overview Dashboard

This page provides a high-level summary of Blinkit's overall business performance.

### Features:

* KPI cards for total sales, ratings, outlets, and products
* Sales distribution by item category
* Outlet size analysis
* Geographic sales funnel
* Year-wise outlet establishment trend
* Interactive slicers for filtering

### Insights Generated:

* Large outlets contribute the highest overall revenue
* Tier 1 cities dominate sales performance
* Fruits & Vegetables generate the highest sales

---

## 2️⃣ Product Analysis Dashboard

This page focuses on detailed product-level analytics.

### Features:

* Treemap visualization for product categories
* Fat content analysis (Regular vs Low Fat)
* Clustered bar charts
* Item category comparison
* Product sales contribution analysis

### Insights Generated:

* Snack Foods and Household Supplies perform strongly
* Low-fat products are growing in Tier 1 cities
* Health-conscious consumers are increasing in urban markets

---

## 3️⃣ Outlet Performance Dashboard

This page evaluates operational performance across all Blinkit outlets.

### Features:

* Outlet ranking by sales
* Outlet type comparison
* Ribbon chart for performance trends
* Average rating comparison
* Geographic outlet analysis

### Insights Generated:

* Certain outlet types consistently outperform others
* High sales do not always guarantee high customer satisfaction
* Some low-performing outlets require operational improvements
